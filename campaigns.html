Host a Blood Drive

Are you an organization, school, or corporate office? Partner with LifeDrop to host a mobile blood drive at your location. We bring the medical staff and equipment; you bring the donors!

Why Host a Drive?

  • ✔️ Great team-building and corporate social responsibility (CSR) activity.
  • ✔️ We handle all logistics, medical screening, and post-donation care.
  • ✔️ You will receive an impact report showing how many lives your team saved.

Recent Milestones

Together, we are making miracles happen every single week. Here is what we accomplished in our recent mega-campaigns.

🏆

World Blood Donor Day

On June 14th, our city-wide drive collected over 1,200 pints in a single weekend, fully restocking 4 major trauma centers.

🚒

First Responders Drive

Partnered with local fire and police departments to raise awareness, welcoming 300 first-time donors into the LifeDrop family.

🎓

College Clash Campaign

A friendly rivalry between three local universities resulted in 850 donations, driven entirely by student volunteers.
