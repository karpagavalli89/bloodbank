About LifeDrop

We are a community-driven blood bank dedicated to bridging the gap between generous donors and patients in critical need. Every drop of blood we collect represents a second chance at life.

Discover Our Purpose ↓

Our Mission & Vision

The Mission

To provide a safe, reliable, and highly efficient blood supply network for hospitals and clinics. We strive to educate the public on the importance of voluntary blood donation and eliminate blood shortages in our region.

The Vision

A world where no life is ever lost due to the unavailability of blood. We envision a society where blood donation is a routine and celebrated act of civic duty, accessible to all.

Our Journey

From a small local drive to a regional lifeline, here is how we grew.

2010

LifeDrop was founded by a group of medical students aiming to organize local emergency blood drives.

2015

Opened our first permanent donation center, allowing us to store and process blood 24/7.

2020

Launched our mobile app and fleet of bloodmobiles, reaching rural communities and saving over 50,000 lives.

Today

Operating 5 major centers and partnering with over 20 regional hospitals to guarantee emergency supplies.

Our Core Values

❤️ Compassion

We treat every donor and recipient with the utmost care, respect, and empathy.

🛡️ Safety First

We adhere to the strictest global medical standards to ensure the safety of both donors and patients.

🤝 Integrity
