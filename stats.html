Where Does Your Blood Go?

Blood is needed for many different reasons. Here is a breakdown of how the blood we collect is typically utilized by our partner hospitals.

Cancer Treatments 35%
General Surgeries 25%
Accidents & Trauma 20%
Maternal & Neonatal Care 15%
Blood Disorders (e.g., Sickle Cell) 5%
