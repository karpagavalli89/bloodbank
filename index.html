About LifeDrop Blood Bank

Founded in 2010, LifeDrop is dedicated to connecting healthy blood donors with patients in urgent need. We maintain a secure, safe, and robust network of regional blood supplies to support local hospitals and clinics.

Our mission is to ensure that no life is lost due to a shortage of blood.

The Donation Process

Donating blood is safe, simple, and takes only about 45 minutes of your time.

1. Registration

Sign in, show ID, and read the required information.

2. Mini-Physical

Check temperature, blood pressure, pulse, and hemoglobin to ensure it is safe for you to donate.

3. The Donation

The actual donation takes about 8-10 minutes. Relax while you save lives.

4. Refreshments

Enjoy a snack and drink for 10-15 minutes before resuming your day.

Who Can Donate?

Before you register to donate, please ensure you meet the basic eligibility criteria:

  • ✅ Must be between 18 and 65 years old.
  • ✅ Must weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • ✅ Must be in good general health and feeling well.
  • ✅ Must not have donated whole blood in the past 56 days.
